# Stage 3: Reflection, Internalization, And Transfer

Use this stage when the user wants reflection, improvement, transfer, or closure after learning.

## Loaded Skills

- `metacognition-questions`
- `transfer-learning`
- `reflection-framework`

## Goal

Help the user inspect how they thought, what they missed, and where else the new understanding can be applied.

## Flow

1. Reflect on what worked and what did not.
2. Ask 2-3 metacognitive questions.
3. Connect the new learning to prior knowledge.
4. Propose at least one transfer scenario.
5. Compare the user's emotional state before and after the learning cycle.

## Metacognition Question Bank

Choose 2-3 questions only.

### Thinking Process

- 你是如何得出这个结论的？
- 在思考过程中，哪个环节最卡壳？
- 有没有哪个时刻你意识到自己可能错了？

### Blind Spots

- 有没有什么信息是你一开始忽略的？
- 如果重来一次，你会先做什么不同的事？
- 你的哪些假设可能被证明是错的？

### Strategy Review

- 你用了什么方法来分析这个问题？
- 这个方法在其他场景也有效吗？
- 有没有更高效的思考方式可以尝试？

### Learning Strategy

- 今天的学习方式对你有效吗？
- 什么帮助你理解得最深？
- 下次遇到类似问题，你会怎么学？

## Reflection Output Pattern

Use a compact structure like this:

```text
【反思与迁移】
过程反思: [这次是如何推进的，哪里有效，哪里低效]
思维盲区: [至少 1 点]
旧知连接: [与已有知识的联系]
迁移场景: [至少 1 个新场景]
下一步实验: [下次可执行的一个动作]
情感复盘: [学习前后状态变化]
```

## Guardrails

- Include both reflection and transfer; do not stop at self-evaluation.
- Avoid overloading the user with too many deep questions in one turn.
- If the user is tired or frustrated, keep the reflection brief and concrete.
